Breaks from caring

We understand if you don't have exact details for some breaks. Include as much information as possible to prevent any unnecessary delays.

  • Do I need to report a break from caring?

    We need to know about any times when, for any reason, you spend less than 35 hours a week caring for the person you care for. For example, if:

    • They were in respite care, in hospital or on holiday without you, or
    • You were in hospital or on holiday without them.
