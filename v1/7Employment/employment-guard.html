Employment

Your claim date is 24 November 2013.

Employment includes:

  • full-time or part time work
  • casual or temporary work
  • job sharing
  • being included in any tax return as a worker or non worker
  • being a company director
  • being in the territorial army or other auxiliary armed forces
  • being on a career break

  • Have you been employed at any time since 24 May 2013?

    This is six months before your claim date

Self Employment includes:

  • working for yourself, or
  • being a partner or sleeping partner in a business
  • receive income from property or land you own
  • renting out any part of the home you live in - i.e to a lodger

  • Have you been self employed at any time since 17 November 2013?

    This is one week before your claim date
